FAQ additions (add at least the first — rest optional)

Question Answer
What happens when I run out of minutes? required Your bill stays flat — we don't charge overage. New calls route to voicemail until the next billing cycle or an upgrade. You'll get email alerts at 75% and 90% of your bucket so you're never surprised.
Can I use one plan across multiple locations? Yes. Minutes are a single pool you can use across any number of locations — we don't track or limit where they're spent. Pick the plan that matches your total monthly call volume.
What if my volume changes month to month? Upgrade or downgrade any time from the weekly email — changes prorate against the current cycle.
Which languages does the agent speak? Greek, English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, and Arabic. The agent locks to the caller's language on the first substantive word of the call.
Can I cancel any time? Yes — no setup fees, no contracts. Cancel from the weekly email or your account. (This matches your existing "Cancel any time" line in the heading.)
